---
title: Uploading Evidence Attachments
description: How to attach verifying documentation to your time logs.
---

Some organizations require you to back up your time entries with screenshots, PDFs, spreadsheet plans, or other verification files. Aika handles these through **Evidence Attachments**.

---

## 1. Supported File Types & Sizes

Before uploading, make sure your files comply with the system limits:

*   **Maximum File Size**: **10 MB** per file.
*   **Supported File Types**:
    *   **Images**: `.png`, `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.webp`
    *   **Documents**: `.pdf`, `.txt`, `.docx`, `.xlsx`, `.pptx`
    *   **Archives**: `.zip`

---

## 2. Uploading Files

You can attach evidence while creating or editing a time log:

1.  Open the **Time Log Dialog** (for a new entry or editing an existing one).
2.  Scroll down to the **Attachments** section.
3.  Drag and drop files into the target zone, or click **Browse Files** to open your system file picker.
4.  **Batch Uploading**: You can select and upload multiple files at once. Aika will process them in parallel.

---

## 3. Managing and Deleting Evidence

*   **Optional Use**: You do not have to attach evidence unless your organization policy requires it. Logs can be saved without files.
*   **Deleting Files**: If you uploaded the wrong file, hover over the attachment card in the log editor and click the **Delete** (trash icon) button.
*   **Batch Deletion**: You can remove multiple attachments in a single batch update before saving the log.
