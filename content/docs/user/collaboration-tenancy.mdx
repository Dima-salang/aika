Aika uses a multi-tenant hierarchy to separate work. You can collaborate within multiple organizations and teams.

---

## 1. Organizations vs. Teams

*   **Organization**: Represents the company, university, or client department (e.g., `"Acme Inc"`). All users, teams, and data are walled within this boundary.
*   **Team**: A group within the organization (e.g., `"Engineering Team"`). Led by a **Team Leader** who can view logs and reports.
*   **Personal View**: If you have no active team in the organization, you operate under your **Personal View** (independent logs).

---

## 2. Onboarding & Invites

You can join organizations and teams in two ways:

### Outbound Invitation Emails
1.  An Admin sends an invitation to your account.
2.  Click the invitation link in your notifications.
3.  Sign in or sign up to automatically join the organization.

### Inbound Magic Links (Join Requests)
1.  A Team Leader or Admin shares a cryptographically secure **Join Link**.
2.  Click the link and log in to request access.
3.  Your request will enter a `pending` state.
4.  An Admin will approve or reject your request in their management dashboard. Once approved, you are automatically added to the organization and targeted team.

---

## 3. Switching Workspaces

To switch between organizations or teams:
1.  Click the **Workspace Selector** dropdown in the top-left corner of the navigation sidebar.
2.  Select the Organization and Team you want to view.

---

## 4. Team Activity Feed

Keep up with team progress in the **Team Activity Feed**:
*   Located on your **Team Dashboard** (for Team Leaders and Managers).
*   Aggregates recent actions, showing completed tasks, created time logs, and evidence updates in a unified chronological stream.
