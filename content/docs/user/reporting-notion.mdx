Aika provides tools to compile your data for supervisor reviews, exports, and external integrations.

---

## 1. Analytics Dashboards & Reports

*   **Personal Reports**: Access these in your sidebar. Select custom date ranges to calculate your total hours, view task project distribution, and inspect time logs.
*   **Team Reports**: If you are a Team Leader or Admin, you can select your team context to view aggregated hour metrics, workload distribution, and progress heatmaps of all members.

---

## 2. Importing & Exporting Data

### Exporting Reports
1.  Navigate to your **Reports** page.
2.  Select the desired date range and team filters.
3.  Click the **Export** button.
4.  Choose your format: **Excel (.xlsx)**, **CSV**, or **PDF**.

### Importing Logs
1.  Click the **Import** button on your dashboard.
2.  Download the template Excel spreadsheet provided.
3.  Fill in the columns with your tasks and time log history.
4.  Upload the spreadsheet to batch-populate your timesheets.

---

## 3. Notion Sync Integration

You can automatically mirror all your Aika time logs directly to your personal Notion workspace:

### Connecting Notion
1.  Go to your User **Settings** and scroll to **Integrations**.
2.  Click **Connect Notion**.
3.  Log into Notion, choose the workspace you want to sync, and authorize permissions.
4.  Aika will automatically generate a parent `"Aika Workspace"` page and create an `"Aika Time Logs"` database inside it.

### Sync Behavior
*   Any log you create, edit, or delete inside Aika will instantly synchronize to your Notion database.
*   **Disconnecting**: You can disconnect the integration at any time from your settings page. All previously synced database logs inside Notion will remain intact.
