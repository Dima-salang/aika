Aika provides two ways to log your work: **Live Timers** and **Manual Logging**. 

---

## 1. Automatic Tracking (Live Timer)

Use the timer to automatically track your current activity as you perform it.

### How to Run the Timer
1.  Locate the **Timer Bar** (visible at the top of your dashboard, or integrated inside your header menu on mobile).
2.  Type a brief description of what you are doing (e.g., `"Refactoring authentication client"`).
3.  Select a project from the project dropdown list (optional).
4.  Click the **Start** button (play icon). The clock will begin counting up.

> [!NOTE]
> **Timer Immunity**: You can reload the page, close your browser, or switch teams/organizations. The timer runs on the backend server and will continue ticking until you return and stop it.

### Stopping and Saving
1.  Click the **Stop** button (square icon).
2.  The time logging dialog will automatically open, pre-filled with your start time, end time, and description.
3.  Link any relevant tasks (see below) and click **Save Log**.

---

## 2. Manual Logging

If you need to enter hours worked in the past:

1.  Click the **Log Time** button on your dashboard.
2.  Input the **Start Date/Time** and **End Date/Time**. Aika will calculate your total duration.
3.  Write a detailed **Description** explaining what you accomplished.
4.  Link tasks or upload evidence files if required.
5.  Click **Save**.

---

## Important Rules & Safeguards

### 🚫 Overlap Prevention
Aika protects you against duplicate logging. You cannot save a time log that overlaps with any previously recorded block of time. If you receive an overlap warning:
*   Double-check your start and end times.
*   Edit the conflicting log in your timeline first if necessary.

### 💾 Local Drafts
If you are filling out a detailed time log or uploading large files and accidentally close the dialog, **your work is not lost**. Aika uses a local browser cache (Zustand) to save your form. Simply open the log dialog again to resume where you left off.
